ABOUT ME


My name is Akhil Emmanuel, and I am a undergraduate engineering student attending Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, NY. I am also an aviation enthusiast and licensed private pilot, with over 100 hours and 250 landings in real life and thousands more in various flight simulators.

Additionally, I have a passion for expressing my creativity through filmmaking. I volunteered at a local county TV station, Montgomery Community Media (MCM), which along with other media classes I took in middle school gave me the skills and knowledge to make proper videos. At MCM I would work behind the scenes in various technical roles, such as the audio board, the teleprompter, the switcher, producer, or the floor director for multiple TV shows. I also produced a pilot episode of my show, DC Tech Zone where I interviewed a technology entrepreneur who came out of the intelligence, military and defense community to highlight the fact that ex-military and intelligence people are capable of starting successful technology companies.

I also enjoy computer science and programming and have made this website from the ground up.
